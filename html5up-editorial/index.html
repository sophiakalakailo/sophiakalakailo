she/they

I'm a journalist with a passion for storytelling. I earned my BA in Journalism and a minor in documentary film from Michigan State University. ​ I am currently the Ypsilanti-area reporter for MLive and the Ann Arbor news. I have primarily worked in statewide reporting for the last couple of years at both Michigan Radio and Bridge Michigan. I covered a variety of topics, including education, environment, housing, and health. ​ Prior to that, I started out as News Editor at The Eastern Echo, Eastern Michigan University’s student newspaper. Upon transferring to Michigan State University my second year, I served as Audience Engagement Editor leading a team of social media content creators, and managing social media accounts for The State News. The following semester, I became City Desk Editor directing coverage on Covid-19, Title IX and city politics.
